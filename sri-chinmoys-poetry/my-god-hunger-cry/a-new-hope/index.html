A New Hope

 &#145;My God-Hunger-Cry’ Prayer for Feb. 17

 
“Every day in my heart-garden
I plant a new hope.
Therefore, in failure-despair-world
I never grope.”
                               Sri Chinmoy

No explanation needed. If we can take that prayer to heart every day
we can create heaven on earth eventually. It may be a good one to
memorize and recite each and every morning. Very significant.

Gratefully,
Arpan

(Originally posted on Sri Chinmoy Inspiration Group)
