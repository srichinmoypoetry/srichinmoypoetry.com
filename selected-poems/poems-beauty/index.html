Poems on beauty

 

You are beautiful, more beautiful, most beautiful,
Beauty unparalleled in the garden of Eden.
Day and night may Thy image abide
In the very depth of my heart.
Without You my eyes have no vision,
Everything is an illusion, everything is barren.
All around me, within and without,
The melody of tenebrous pangs I hear.
My world is filled with excruciating pangs.
O Lord, O my beautiful Lord,
O my Lord of beauty, in this lifetime
Even for a fleeting second,
May I be blessed with the boon
To see Thy Face.

Sri Chinmoy, My Flute, Agni Press, 1972

Appreciate beauty,
Only to become
Beauty itself.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 37,

Beauty
Is my heart’s
Mind-transcendence-hope.

- Sri Chinmoy

My heart’s aspiration-beauty-flames
Are destined to climb
The transcendental Heights
Of the Beyond.

- Sri Chinmoy

When I look at a child,
He gives me
His soul-beauty-dreams.

- Sri Chinmoy

Eternity’s Beauty
Is in my mind’s
Self-control.
Infinity’s Beauty
Is in my life’s
Transformation-goal.

- Sri Chinmoy

You are God’s Beauty:
Beauty human,
Beauty divine,
Beauty supreme.
This lofty truth shall forever remain unchallenged.

- Sri Chinmoy

Earth’s beauty
Is in its soulfully smiling hope.
Heaven’s beauty
Is in its peacefully loving promise.

- Sri Chinmoy

 

Power
Frightens us.

Beauty
Enlightens us.

- Sri Chinmoy Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 40 #39511, #39512

The outer beauty
Dies.
The inner beauty
Prevails.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 23

Love is beauty’s
Sweet touch.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 35

Beautiful, more beautiful,
Most beautiful —
The golden disc in the eastern firmament
Early in the morning!

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 40

 

Writing on Beauty

“The poet Keats said, “A thing of beauty is a joy forever.” But what is that thing? That thing, from the spiritual point of view, is our soul. The soul is eternally beautiful. God expresses Himself in the physical through beauty, pure beauty, beauty without a blemish. This beauty is not merely skin-deep, but it is in the soul. Here the soul is expressing itself through the physical form.”

Excerpt from Rainbow-Flowers by Sri Chinmoy.
