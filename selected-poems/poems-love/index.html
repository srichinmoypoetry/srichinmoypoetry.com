Poems about Love

 

 

Love is not a thing to understand.
Love is not a thing to feel.
Love is not a thing to give and receive.
Love is a thing only to become
And eternally be.

- Sri Chinmoy, When God love descends

The most effective medicine
Here on earth
Is love unconditional.

- Sri Chinmoy, My Christmas-New Year-Vacation Aspiration-Prayers, part 32

Those who can live without love
Are not ready
For the world of aspiration.

- Sri Chinmoy Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 45

At least for a day,
Let me love, only love,
Only love the world!

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 37

When
You talk about
Your wasted love,
You just increase
Your blind ignorance.

Love
Is never wasted.
Love
Can never be wasted,
For love is Infinity’s Life.

- Sri Chinmoy The Dance of Life, part 18

The hands of
Power
Are often destructive.
The hands of
Love
Are always creative.

- Sri Chinmoy, Power and love

Love the world
With your heart’s dreams.
Serve the world
With your life’s promises.

- Sri Chinmoy, A love-bathed heart

No remedy for black hate?
Who says?
Just love
And love more,
That’s all.

- Sri Chinmoy (excerpt from: Wings of Light, part 10, #492)

Love expands,
Love magnifies,
Love beautifies
And love unifies
A seeker’s heart.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 2

One word that wakes
All humanity:
Love, love, love!

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 6

Think not, my mind.
Just love and love
And love God.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 25

The heart
Just loves and loves
And loves the truth.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, Part 28,

 

Eternity was born
Of Love.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 36,

What is love divine?
Love divine is
Something that never loses hope.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 32

Love and live
And live and love
Each moment
To the fullest.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 38

Not a shallow love
But a deep love
Binds the world-citizens
Together.

Sri Chinmoy, Seventy-Seven Thousand Service-Trees, part 49

Human love
Either unconsciously or consciously
Loves confines.
Divine love
Knows no boundaries.

Sri Chinmoy, Twenty-Seven Thousand Aspiration-Plants, part 261,

 Quotes about Love

“Hate is often an obverse form of love.
You hate someone whom you really wish to love but whom you cannot love.”

Sri Chinmoy, Earth’s cry meets heaven’s smile, part 3, Agni Press, 1978
