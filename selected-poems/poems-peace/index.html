Poems about Peace

 

Expect not!
Suspect not!
Lo, peace is all yours.

Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Heart-Home, part 1

My inner peace
Does not select anybody,
Does not reject anybody.
My inner peace
Always self-givingly projects itself.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Fragrance-Heart, part 1

O dreamers of peace, come.
Let us walk together.
O lovers of peace, come,
Let us run together.
O servers of peace, come.
Let us grow together.

Sri Chinmoy, No unreachable goal

We can change the world,
But not improve it,
If we do not have peace.

Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Heart-Home, part 1

Peace is in
The fragrance-heart
Of my sleepless meditation.

- Sri Chinmoy, A heart of oneness-peace

Shun the very breath of negativity!
Lo, your mind has become
The possessor of peace.

Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Heart-Home, part 1

God asks me every morning,
“My child,
Is it such a difficult task
To say a prayer for world-peace?”

Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Heart-Home, part 1

Peace I feel
In infinite measure
When my life becomes
The fragrance
Of my gratitude-heart.

- Sri Chinmoy, A heart of oneness-peace

I receive peace
In abundant measure
When I take my mind
To fly with me
In my simplicity, sincerity,
Humility and purity-plane
In the Sky of God’s Compassion,
Protection and Satisfaction

- Sri Chinmoy, A heart of oneness-peace

Peace
Is not the fault-finder
Of the world.
Peace
Is the fault-mender
Of the world.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace-Blossom-Fragrance, part 6

God has infinite children,
But the name of His fondest child
Is Peace.

- Sri Chinmoy, A heart of peace

 

Peace
Is the vision-beauty
Of ancient dawns.
Peace
Is the vision-dream-choice
Of modern noons.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace-Blossom-Fragrance, part 6

Peace does not mean the absence of war.
Peace means the presence of harmony,
Love, oneness and satisfaction.
Peace means a flood of love
In the world-family.

Sri Chinmoy, A heart of peace

Peace
Silences the nightmares of the mind.
Peace
Feeds the dreams of the heart.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Heart-Home, part 2

World-peace
Can be achieved
When the power of love
Replaces
The love of power.

- Sri Chinmoy,  Peace-Lovers, AUM publications

Power is the most difficult thing
To work with.
Peace is the easiest thing
To work with
And also the most rewarding.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Fragrance-Heart, part 2

Do not allow the past
To impose itself on you.
Do not allow the future
To come to you unduly fast.
Just try to live
In your today’s peace-world.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Fragrance-Heart, part 2

God has not forgotten
To give us peace.
He is just waiting for us
To ask for it.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Fragrance-Heart, part 2

Poor is he
Who does not have peace.
Poorer is he
Who does not need peace.
Poorest is he
Who does not want to hear
People talking about peace.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace: God’s Fragrance-Heart, part 2

My heart dreams of peace,
while my mind dreams of supremacy.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

May the peace I feel in my imagination
become a reality in my life.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

 

Peace can make me smile
even through my multiplying tears.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Peace is a stranger to the rigid mind.
Peace is a guest of the flexible heart.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Peace will always treasure and safeguard
your world-oneness-heart.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

In Heaven, peace has another name: light.
On earth, peace has another name: love.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Peace of the compromising minds is mortal.
Peace of the sacrificing hearts is immortal.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

If you have inner peace,
nobody can force you to be a slave to the outer reality.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Patience, patience, patience.
What you need is patience.
In the heart of your patience you will discover peace-blooms and satisfaction-blossoms.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Peace-dreamer I was. Peace-lover I am.
Peace-giver I shall eventually become.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

The real in me longs for peace.
The unreal in me longs for power.

- Sri Chinmoy, Peace God’s Fragrance Heart

Quotes on Peace

What does peace do? Peace blossoms. What else? Peace spreads. What else? Peace illumines. What else? Peace fulfils.

– Sri Chinmoy, Peace.
