The Ideal Disciple

The ideal disciple
Is he who believes
Before he sees.

The ideal disciple
Is he whose mind
Is inside his heart.

The ideal disciple
Is he whose heart
Lives for his soul.

The ideal disciple
Is he whose soul
Distributes his goal.

Excerpt from ‘The Wings of Light Part 1‘ by  Sri Chinmoy
