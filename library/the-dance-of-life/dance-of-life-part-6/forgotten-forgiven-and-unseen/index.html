Forgotten, Forgiven And Unseen

O forgotten yesterdays,
Take back, take back
Your sorrow-treasure.

O forgiven todays,
Go back, go back
To your home of night.

O unseen tomorrows,
Come soon, come soon
To awaken my ignorance-dream.
