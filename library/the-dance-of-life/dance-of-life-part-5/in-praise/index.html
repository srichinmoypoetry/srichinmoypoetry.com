In Praise

In praise of women
I must say
They love and they want to be loved.

In praise of men
I must say
They want to be loved only.

In praise of God
I must say
He is trying to live
Without human love.

Excerpt from ‘Dance of Life Part 5′ by  Sri Chinmoy
