The Swimmer

He swims
In the ocean of hope.
He swims
In the ocean of failure
He swims
In the ocean of tears.
Something more:
He swims also
In the ocean of his surrendered will
To his earth-Heaven-life’s Pilot Supreme.

Excerpt from ‘Transcendence-Perfection‘ by  Sri Chinmoy
